# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 2026

## Data We Collect

- **Account Information**: Email address and display name when you sign up (Google Sign-In or email/password).
- **Task Data**: All tasks, templates, notes, and settings you create within the app.
- **Usage Data**: Anonymous crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics.

## How We Use Your Data

- To provide and sync your task data across devices.
- To send push notifications for task reminders (optional).
- To improve app stability through crash reporting.

## Data Storage

All data is stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore (Google Cloud Platform, us-central region). We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

## Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Account Profile > Delete Account. This permanently removes all tasks, templates, and profile data.

## Contact

For privacy concerns, open an issue at: https://github.com/justaman045/agentic-todo
